Optical Illusion

An Exploration of Accomplishing an Optical Illusion with p5.js

Play / View It Here

About This Work

This optical illusion uses depth and contrast, rendered with p5.js, to reproduce a well-known optical illusion. This illusion is essentially created by rotating and shrinking layers of a checkerboard pattern. It is static, but if the user focuses their gaze on the image long enough, it creates the illusion of movement.
