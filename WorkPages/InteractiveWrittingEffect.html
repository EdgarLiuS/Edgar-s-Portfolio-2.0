About This Work

This is one of my latest P5 projects. All writing effects are calculated in real time. Using the TextToPoints function in P5.js, I was able to manipulate text effects with greater precision. This project sorts each character, allowing them to appear and be rendered sequentially when the user clicks the mouse, and gradually erased when the mouse is released. In addition, I implemented a procedural layout to ensure that line widths remain constant regardless of whether they are enlarged or reduced: lines with more characters shrink, and lines with fewer characters enlarge.

P.S. Press and hold your mouse to begin writing!

Keywords: graphic-design, procedural-generation, animated
