About This Work

P5 Poster was one of my early P5 projects; it was a group project. Another classmate and I were in a group, and we each drew a picture on paper, which we then had to copy in P5. It was a challenge for me at the time, but this project helped me learn a considerable amount about P5 features and statements.

Keywords: procedural-generation, static, sketch
