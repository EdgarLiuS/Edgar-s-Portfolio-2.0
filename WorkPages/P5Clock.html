About This Work

A P5 Clock is an exploration of the possibilities in clock design. A clock, as a tool for measuring time, can be limited to any form of expression, as long as it fulfills its function of telling time. Virtual clocks expand this further, freeing themselves from the constraints of reality. The design of a virtual clock can be more than just a set of numbers or a simple simulation of a real clock. Through this project, I explored the possibilities of clock design, using more abstract forms to represent the hour, minute, and second hands: the second hand is a rippling wave, the minute hand is two rectangles whose length and width change to indicate minutes, and the hour hand uses a minimal triangle as the background to display the time. Furthermore, I added vibrant colors to the elements in this work and blended them using Multiply blending mode to reduce the monotony of the minimalist graphics.

Keywords: graphic-design, procedural-generation, animated
